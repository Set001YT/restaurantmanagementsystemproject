Our Story

Founded in 2015, Delicious Bites has been serving exceptional cuisine to the heart of Astana for over 10 years. What started as a small family-owned restaurant has grown into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations.

Our philosophy is simple: use only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients to create dishes that not only satisfy your hunger but also touch your soul. Every plate that leaves our kitchen is a testament to our commitment to kazakh culinary excellence.

We believe that dining is an experience, not just a meal. From the moment you walk through our doors, our team is dedicated to making your visit memorable, whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a casual dinner with friends.
